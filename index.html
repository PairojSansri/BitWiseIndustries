How it works

SansriCreative has a process that provides consistent results to maxize your brand recogognition, S.E.O., and web traffic in three basic steps:

  • Intial Meeting

    In the intial meeting, we will have the chance introduce ourselves, your business and it's story. We believe that in order to truly have a website that reflects both you and the business, it is vital to get to know both. Next, we will discuss expectations, objectives and needs from eachother, any key information or visuals needed to highlight, as well as logos and color schemes.

  • Draft Choices

    A short time afterward clients recieve a wireframe of potential designs and color scheme options. Once a design and color scheme is approved, a draft will be completed. While a draft is in progress, clients will use this time to gether resources they may want to utilize such as videos and high quality photos.

  • Finalization

    Once approval of the overall design of the desktop version is gained, animations and mobile formats will be added for final submission. Publishment on final approval.
